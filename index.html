ABOUT US

SASTRA is one of India’s premier institutions, offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in Engineering, Management, Law, Sciences, Humanities, and Education. It received Deemed-to-be-University status in 2001 from the University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 3 of the UGC Act 19562.

Srinivasa Ramanujan Centre (SRC):

Located in Kumbakonam, this off-campus center is a tribute to the renowned mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan. Kumbakonam, named after the presiding deity Lord Aadhi Kumbeshwarar, is steeped in South Indian culture. It’s famous for its temples, intricately carved panchaloha idols, exquisite brasswares (especially lamps), silk, and betel leaves.
